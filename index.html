About me

Building things that matter.

Hi, I'm Alex — a product designer and full-stack developer based in San Francisco. I believe the best digital products feel inevitable: intuitive, fast, and beautiful.

With a background spanning UX research, frontend engineering, and brand identity, I bridge the gap between how things look and how they work. I've shipped products used by millions and collaborated with teams across four continents.

When I'm not staring at a screen, you'll find me hiking coastal trails, experimenting with film photography, or obsessing over a perfect cup of pour-over coffee.
